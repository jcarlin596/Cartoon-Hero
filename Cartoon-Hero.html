About Ralph

Ralph is a lovable game avatar with a heart as big as a mountain, but he is as clumsy as a bull in a china shop. During his adventures he travels the length of the games arcdae, causing mayhem and mischief whereever he goes until he eventually discovers the secret to becoming one of the good guys.
